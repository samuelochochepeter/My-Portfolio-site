About Me

My name is Samuel Ochoche Peter, a Computer Science graduate and growth-focused Technology Professional with experience in Web Development, Data Science, Digital Marketing and IT Training. I enjoy using technology to solve real-life business problems and have successfully built and delivered systems for real estate, pharmacy, e-commerce and smart management use cases.

I work with tools such as Python, HTML, CSS, PHP, MySQL, Bootstrap, Excel and Power BI to design data-driven solutions, and I also help individuals and small businesses grow their brands through effective Digital Marketing and Social Media Management.

In addition to developing systems, I teach and mentor students in Python programming and data analytics, helping them to build relevant tech skills and start their own career journeys. My goal is to continue building scalable digital solutions while contributing to business growth and digital transformation in Nigeria and beyond.
